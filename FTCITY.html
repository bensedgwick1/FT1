City-Level Unemployment Estimates by Field

An estimated breakdown of unemployed graduates for major cities within all 50 U.S. states.

EXTREMELY IMPORTANT: READ BEFORE PROCEEDING

The data on this page is a multi-layered statistical estimate. Official government data for unemployment by college major at the city level DOES NOT EXIST.

These figures are generated by a model. For most states, the model applies national averages. A fine-tuned model has been applied to Washington State, using local demographic and economic data to provide a more precise estimate, including an estimated working population based on state-wide age distribution.
