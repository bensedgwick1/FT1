Maximizing Your Travel Budget

Travel During Shoulder Season

Avoid peak tourist times to get 30-50% discounts on accommodations and activities.

Eat Like a Local

Street food and local restaurants often offer the best value and most authentic experiences.

Use Local Transportation

Buses, trains, and ride-sharing apps are typically much cheaper than taxis or rental cars.
